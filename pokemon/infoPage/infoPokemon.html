Pokémon: Gotta catch 'em all!

Pokémon is a video game centered on this fictional creatures, developed by Game Freak. In this game you can catch and train your own Pokémon team, and make them battle against other Pokémon Trainers teams. Nowadays, there are 8 generations of Pokémon main video games, and a lot of worldwide competitions.
